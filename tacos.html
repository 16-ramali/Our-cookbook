Ingredients:

- Ground beef (or ground turkey, chicken, or beans for a vegetarian option): 1 lb
- Taco seasoning: 1 packet (or homemade blend of chili powder, cumin, paprika, and salt)
- Tortillas: 8-10 corn or flour tortillas
- Shredded cheese (Monterey Jack or Cheddar work well): 1 cup
- Diced tomatoes: 1 cup
- Diced onions: 1/2 cup
- Diced bell peppers: 1/2 cup
- Sour cream: 1/2 cup
- Salsa: 1/2 cup
- Cilantro: chopped, for garnish
- Lettuce: chopped, for garnish

Instruction:

1. Brown the ground beef: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
2. Add taco seasoning: Once the beef is browned, add the taco seasoning packet (or homemade blend) and stir to combine. Cook for 1-2 minutes, until the flavors are fragrant.
3. Prepare toppings: While the beef is cooking, prepare the toppings. Chop the diced tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers. Crumble the shredded cheese.
4. Warm tortillas: Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
5. Assemble tacos: To assemble the tacos, spoon some of the seasoned ground beef onto a warmed tortilla, followed by a sprinkle of shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers.
6. Add sour cream and salsa: Top each taco with a dollop of sour cream and a spoonful of salsa.
7. Garnish with cilantro and lettuce: Garnish each taco with chopped cilantro and lettuce.

Variations:

- Carne Asada Tacos: Replace ground beef with grilled steak, sliced into thin strips.
- Chicken Tacos: Replace ground beef with shredded or diced cooked chicken.
- Veggie Tacos: Replace ground beef with roasted or saut√©ed vegetables, such as bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms.
- Breakfast Tacos: Replace ground beef with scrambled eggs, crumbled bacon or sausage, and shredded cheese.
