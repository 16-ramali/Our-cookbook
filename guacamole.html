Guacamole Recipe

next recipe
Mexican main page

A classic Mexican dip made with ripe avocados, onions, lime juice, and spices.

Ingredients:

- Avocados: 3-4 ripe
- Onions: 1 medium, finely chopped
- Lime juice: 2-3 tbsp
- Salt: to taste
- Cilantro: 1/4 cup, chopped (optional)
- Garlic: 1-2 cloves, minced (optional)
- Tomatoes: 1 medium, diced (optional)

Instructions:

1. Cut and pit the avocados: Cut the avocados in half and remove the pit. Scoop the flesh into a large mixing bowl.
2. Add the onions, lime juice, and salt: Add the chopped onions, lime juice, and salt to the bowl with the avocado flesh.
3. Mash the ingredients: Use a fork to mash the ingredients together until you reach your desired consistency. Some people like their guacamole smooth, while others prefer it chunky.
4. Add the cilantro, garlic, and tomatoes (if using): Stir in the chopped cilantro, minced garlic, and diced tomatoes (if using).
5. Taste and adjust: Taste the guacamole and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
6. Cover and refrigerate: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the guacamole for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Tips and Variations:

- Use ripe avocados for the best flavor and texture.
- Adjust the amount of lime juice and salt to your taste.
- Add a diced jalape√±o or serrano pepper for an extra kick of heat.
- Mix in some chopped fresh herbs like parsley or basil for added flavor.
- Serve the guacamole with tortilla chips, on tacos, or as a dip for vegetables.
