MassWadi

Ingredients:

For the Maswadi Filling:
 1 cup besan (gram flour)
2 tbsp dry coconut (grated)
1 tbsp sesame seeds
1 tbsp poppy seeds (khus khus)
2 tbsp roasted peanut powder
2 tbsp coriander leaves (finely chopped)
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp cumin powder
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
Salt to taste
2 tbsp oil
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
For the Outer Covering (Optional, if making rolls instead of just stuffing):
 1 cup wheat flour or multigrain flour
Water, as needed to knead the dough
1 tsp oil
Salt to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Roast and Prepare the Filling
 1. Heat a pan and dry roast sesame seeds, poppy seeds, dry coconut, and peanut powder until golden brown. Let it cool and grind coarsely.
2. In the same pan, heat 2 tbsp oil, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter. Add a pinch of hing.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chilies. Saut√© for a minute.
4. Add besan and roast it well until aromatic (about 5-7 minutes) on low flame.
5. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, sugar, and salt.
6. Add the roasted coconut-peanut mixture and mix everything well.
7. Turn off the flame and add lemon juice and chopped coriander. Allow the mixture to cool.
Step 2: Roll and Cut the Maswadi
 1. Take a clean surface or rolling board, grease it lightly, and spread the besan mixture into a thin layer.
2. Roll it tightly into a log shape and press gently.
3. Cut into small bite-sized rolls or slices.
Step 3: Optional - Cover with Outer Layer
 1. If making a covering, knead a soft dough with wheat flour, salt, and water.
2. Roll out small chapati-sized sheets, place the besan mixture inside, and roll it tightly.
3. Cut into pieces and lightly roast them on a tawa with a little oil.

Serving Suggestions:

Serve Maswadi with spicy amti (Maharashtrian dal), kadhi, or green chutney.
It pairs well with steamed rice and ghee.
