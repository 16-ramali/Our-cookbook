Ingredients

For the Chicken:

2 lbs (900g) chicken wings or boneless thighs (cut into bite-size pieces)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon ginger powder (or 1 tsp grated fresh ginger)
- ½ cup cornstarch or potato starch
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 egg (optional, for extra crispiness)
- ½ cup cold water (adjust as needed)
- Oil for deep frying (vegetable, canola, or peanut oil)

For the Sauce:

- 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons honey (or brown sugar)
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar (or apple cider vinegar)
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 3 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 teaspoon grated ginger
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for extra spice)
- 1 tablespoon butter (for a glossy finish)
- 2 tablespoons water (to thin the sauce if needed)

For Garnish:

- Sesame seeds
- Green onions (chopped)

Procedure

1. Prep the Chicken
1. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and ginger powder.
2. In a bowl, mix cornstarch, flour, egg (if using), and cold water to make a light batter.
3. Coat each piece of chicken in the batter evenly.

2. First Fry (for tenderness)
1. Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer to 325°F (163°C).
2. Fry chicken in batches for 5-7 minutes until pale golden. Remove and drain on a rack or paper towels.
3. Second Fry (for extra crispiness)

1. Increase oil temperature to 375°F (190°C).
2. Fry chicken again for 2-3 minutes until deep golden brown and crispy.
3. Drain excess oil.
4. Make the Sauce

1. In a pan, heat sesame oil and melt butter.
2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 30 seconds.
3. Stir in gochujang, ketchup, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and red pepper flakes.
4. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until thickened.

5. Coat & Serve
1. Toss fried chicken in the sauce until fully coated.
2. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
3. Serve immediately and enjoy!


Serving Suggestions:

- Serve with pickled radish(danmuji).
- Serve with steamed rice.
- Serve with cold barley tea.


Tips and Variations:

- Double fry for extra crunch
- Use potato starch for a lighter,crispier coating.
- Toss in sauce right before serving.
- Use more gochujang & chilli flakes for extra spicieness.
